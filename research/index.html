Home Research Teaching CV Contact

My research interests include the political economy of communication & media, critical communication research, ICTs, digital labor & gig worker, platform economy, algorithmic management, human-computer/AI interaction, algorithmic fairness, accountability, transparency, and ethics (FATE), and mixed methods research.

🎉 I am offered a position as an Assessment of Learning and Teaching (ALT) Fellow. I anticipate to examine and design experiential learning in pedagogy to promote belongingness during the summer and fall of 2025.

🎉 I received the 2025 TIPI Graduate Student Research Fellowship for the project titled “Outsourcing Invisibility: How AI Organizations Communicatively Constitute Data Annotation as Non-Work”. I will spend the rest of 2025 working on this project!

🎉 I received the 2023 Dallas Smythe Award at IAMCR for the project titled “Digital Capital Never Sleeps: An Empirical Study of Digital Labor and Manufacturing Consent in China”.
