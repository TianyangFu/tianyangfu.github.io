My name is Tianyang Fu (she/they). I am a 1st year PhD student in Communication Studies (Organizational Communication and Technology) at Moody College of Communication, the University of Texas at Austin.

I am exploring how digital media and information communication technologies (ICTs) represent and participate in the power interaction between the state, market, and society while highlighting their mutual embeddedness and everlasting conflict.

I obtained a B.A. in Journalism (Intercultural Communication) at Beijing Foreign Studies University with a minor in International Economics and Trade, and later an M.A. in Social Sciences at the University of Chicago with a concentration on sociology.

I received the 2023 Dallas Smythe Award.

✏️ I am currently serving as the coordinator for an article collection in the Organizational Communication section of the open access journal Frontiers in Communication. The collection, tentatively titled “Feminist Organizational Communication Studies: Engaging Gender with Algorithms at Work,” aims to explore the intersections of gender, algorithms, and organizational communication.

We are currently seeking guest editors and article contributors who are interested in these themes. If this topic resonates with your research interests, we warmly invite you to reach out via the email above.
